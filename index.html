Frequently Asked Questions

Who can donate blood?

Generally, donors must be 18-65 years old, weigh at least 50kg, and be in good health. Specific eligibility criteria are checked during registration.

How does the matching work?

Our AI considers blood type compatibility, donor location, recent donation history, and health parameters to find the most suitable donors.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and never share your data without consent. Hospitals only see necessary donor information.
